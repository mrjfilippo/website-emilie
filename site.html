Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about life coaching.

Life coaching is a collaborative partnership where I help you identify your goals, overcome obstacles, and create actionable strategies to achieve the life you want. Unlike therapy which often focuses on healing past wounds, coaching is future-oriented and action-focused, helping you bridge the gap between where you are and where you want to be.

While therapy often focuses on diagnosing and treating mental health conditions and healing from past trauma, coaching is future-focused and action-oriented. Coaching assumes you're fundamentally whole and capable, and helps you maximize your potential, set and achieve goals, and create the life you want. Many clients find coaching and therapy to be complementary.

The duration varies depending on your goals and commitment level. Most clients see meaningful progress within 3-6 months of weekly or bi-weekly sessions. Some clients achieve their initial goals quickly and choose to continue coaching to work on new areas, while others find a few months of coaching gives them the tools they need to continue independently.

My approach is warm, direct, and tailored to your unique needs. I blend evidence-based techniques from positive psychology, cognitive behavioral approaches, and mindfulness with intuitive coaching. Sessions are collaborative - I'll ask powerful questions, challenge limiting beliefs, help you gain clarity, and support you in taking purposeful action. You'll always leave with clear next steps.

All sessions are currently conducted via secure video call, which allows me to work with clients worldwide and provides greater scheduling flexibility. Many clients find virtual sessions just as effective as in-person, with the added convenience of participating from the comfort of their own space. All you need is a reliable internet connection and a private space for our sessions.
